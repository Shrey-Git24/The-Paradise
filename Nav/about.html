A PASSION TO SERVE

Four simple words comprise The Paradise's mission statement:

WHERE IT ALL BEGAN...

Our founder, Sarthak Holmes, had an idea to create a restaurant that was more than a place that served great food. Sarthak wanted to give locals a place where they’d always feel at home. A place where they could get no-nonsense food at down-to-earth prices, and where they’d be treated right by people who had a passion to serve. The culture at The Paradise restaurant was instilled by Sarthak long before a mission statement was ever written down on paper and is still evident today. At The Paradise, guests, team members and community are all treated with respect.

SOMETHING FOR EVERYONE

We’re loved for our special Basket Chaat, our signature Cheese Burst Pizza, our famous Malai Lassi and more. All are served in a distinctly hearty heavenly style, enjoyed over good times with great family and friends. Simple, honest, genuine food. Never pretentious. Always satisfying.

ALWAYS THE REAL DEAL

Our inviting, comfortable dining room is the perfect place to enjoy a great meal. Our grab a seat at our neighborhood community tables provide an energetic and lively atmosphere that is perfect for our famous Thursday Night Trivia. The Paradise is headquartered in Lucknow and is proud to serve more than 20 million guests a year in over 100 restaurants throughout the World. We will continue to prosper and grow and will continue to take care of every guest who walks through our doors, just like we’ve always done. We treat people right, and that’s what makes new guests feel welcome, and regulars feel right at home.
